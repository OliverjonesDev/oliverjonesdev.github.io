    This game was my second large project that I developed in the span of 5-6 months, from late december to early may. I learnt alot about game development, pacing and what I should focus on. In this project I spent too much time on the visuals and didn't flesh out the mechanics enough. Because of this in late april I had to rebuild the game from the ground up and this caused complications with the brief and due to negligence on my behalf caused me a lower grade then I would of liked on this submission.

    This has taught me how valuable playtesting is, the development time line for these kind of projects and how creating half baking systems can bite you later on. So overall this was an amazing learning experience and I will not be making these same mistakes in my next games.
